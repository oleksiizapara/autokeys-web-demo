about us

Auto Island LTD (also known as Auto Keys) is a locally-owned automotive diagnostic and locksmith company serving the Christchurch and Canterbury regions since 2009. We specialize in automotive diagnostics, vehicle servicing, general repairs, and locksmith services.

In addition to our locksmith services, Auto Keys offers comprehensive mechanical repairs and vehicle health checks. Our skilled technicians focus on preventative maintenance to keep your vehicle running smoothly and efficiently.

Need expert automotive advice? Contact us today, and our team of experienced technicians will be happy to assist with any questions you have.

Our Mission And Goal

Auto Keys Christchurch specializes in providing comprehensive automotive key replacement services. Whether you’ve lost your car keys or need a spare key cut and transponder programmed, our expert team is here to assist you. We offer on-site programming for the latest transponder keys for most vehicle makes and models. Please note, some vehicles may require additional time or bench programming.

In addition to key replacement, Auto Keys also offers mechanical services and vehicle repairs, including preventative maintenance to keep your vehicle running smoothly. We cater to both individual customers and fleet servicing needs.

Contact us today, and our skilled automotive technicians will be happy to answer any questions or schedule a service.
